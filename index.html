Tanjiro Kamado

Bio

Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his younger sister, Nezuko, is turned into a demon.

Abilities

Tanjiro possesses enhanced senses and incredible strength due to his training as a demon slayer. He is skilled in swordsmanship and water breathing techniques.
